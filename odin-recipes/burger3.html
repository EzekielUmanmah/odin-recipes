Burger

A burger made from bacon, eggs, and avocado

A delicious melt-in-your-mouth burger made from the tastiest ingredients

ingredients

  • 100% beef patty
  • bun
  • bacon
  • avocado
  • egg

Steps

  1. Grill the patty
  2. Toast the bun
  3. Fry the egg
  4. Dice some avocado
  5. Put it all together
